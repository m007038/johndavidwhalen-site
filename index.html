Why strategy?

Intelligence became abundant. Connection did not.

The premium has moved to the human side of the table.

Every strategy deck, every analysis, every plan can now be generated in seconds. When everyone holds the same intelligence, it stops being an advantage. What remains scarce is the leader who reads a room, the brand that earns belief, the institution people trust.

That scarcity is our practice. SYF Consulting works both sides of the divide. We build agentic AI systems that execute the mechanical parts of a market strategy at machine speed. And we train the human parts machines cannot reach: the hard conversation, the narrative that earns belief, the judgment call no model can make for you.

The result is not people who sound like machines. It is machines doing machine work and people who are unmistakably, persuasively themselves. Ask yourself: when the work is automated, what will your people be worth in a room?

How I think

The method, shown in public.

Since March 2021 I have consulted on brand strategy for a major space travel company, cities, and other organizations. Most of that work is protected by NDA. These public cases show the thinking.

M[IAM]I

Place branding · City of Miami
Problem

Miami is perceived as the branded house of the South Beach party scene, when it is really a house of brands built from dozens of artistic communities.

Insight

Everyone is an artist in Miami.

Strategy

Inspire the artist within people by connecting them to the art around them.

Solution

We launched M[IAM]I across several mediums, with a different resident artistic competition sustaining the brand each year.

KFC Spain

Campaign strategy · M.AD
Problem

Post pandemic, Spain suffered a series of social crises. We were asked to address one and build a campaign around "first chances."

Insight

Spanish people are exhausted by the vicious circle of post-pandemic loneliness. Sometimes we need a little help from our friends.

Strategy

Invite people to rediscover the healing power of community with KFC.

Solution

American homestyle cooking already reads as the center of community. We created KFC catered Plaza Parties with games and activities to restart local celebration.
